Our story

TastyBites started in 2010 as a small family-run bistro. Over the years we have grown while staying true to our core: seasonality, local sourcing and a human approach to hospitality. We partner with small farms that practise responsible animal welfare and sustainable agriculture.

Philosophy

We keep things simple: use the best ingredients, respect seasonality, support local producers and make dishes that highlight natural flavours. This philosophy guides menu development and service culture across the restaurant.

Meet the Chef

Marco Rossi

Marco trained in Bologna and Paris and returned to London to open TastyBites. His cooking blends classic technique with seasonal British produce. He leads menu design and kitchen coaching.

Sourcing & Sustainability

We source directly from Ridgeview Farm for vegetables, Riverbank Fisheries for sustainably-caught fish, and GreenBarn for dairy. Packaging is compostable and we monitor waste to minimise our footprint.
